  • Game Rules And Hints
  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By Clicking on the crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each crystals is hidden from you until you click on.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystals.
